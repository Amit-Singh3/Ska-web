About Us

Welcome to SKA - Mi Amor,
the premier dramatics society of Deenbandhu Chhotu Ram University of Science and Technology (DCRUST), Murthal.
Our name, "SKA" means "different from the crowd" and "Mi Amor," translates to "My Love", embodying the passion and dedication we pour into every performance and artistic endeavor.

At SKA - Mi Amor,
we are a vibrant collection of creative individuals united by our love for the performing arts. Our society encompasses a diverse range of talents and disciplines, including acting, dance, music, and writing. We believe that the stage is a powerful platform for expression, storytelling, and connecting with audiences.

Our mission is to foster a supportive and inclusive environment where students can explore their artistic potential, develop their skills, and collaborate on a variety of projects. We strive to create experiences that resonate with both participants and spectators.
